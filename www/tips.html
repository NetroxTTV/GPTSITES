Tips & Tricks

Here's some quick tips to help you earn money this holiday season!

1

Start with High-Paying Sites

Focus on sites with 80%+ rates like Earnlab, CashinStyle, and GemsLoot. These offer the best return for your time.

2

Game Offers Pay Well

Mobile game offers often pay $5-$50+. Look for games that require reaching a specific level - these usually have the best payouts.

3

Check for Bonuses

Many sites offer special holiday bonuses, streak rewards, and Christmas promotions. Log in daily to maximize your earnings this season!

4

Compare Offerwalls

The same offer can pay differently on different sites. Use our Offerwalls page to find which site pays the most for each offer.

5

Use a Dedicated Email

Create a separate email for GPT sites to keep your inbox organized and avoid missing important reward notifications.

6

Avoid VPNs

Most sites will ban accounts using VPNs. Always complete offers from your real location to avoid losing your earnings.

7

Screenshot Everything

Always take screenshots of completed offers. This helps when submitting support tickets for missing credits.

8

Focus on One Offer at a Time

Complete one offer fully before starting another. This helps avoid confusion and ensures you get credited properly.
