Offer Overview

Eatventure is a restaurant idle/tycoon game where you build and upgrade food businesses to grow income and expand to new cities. This is one of the highest-paying offers available with rewards ranging from $200-500 depending on your region.

High Reward Alert: While this offer requires time investment (50-90 hours), the payout makes it one of the most profitable offers available. Perfect for players who enjoy idle/tycoon games!

How The Game Works

Start Small
You start with a small food stand and earn money by serving customers
Upgrade Everything
  • Food stations
  • Workers
  • Speed
  • Profit multipliers
Progress Through Cities
Complete a restaurant → Move to the next one. Progress through cities to unlock higher earnings
Snowball Effect
The more you upgrade, the faster you snowball and the faster you complete cities

Vault System

The Vault contains cards that give permanent boosts when leveled up (e.g., tips, coin boosts, speed). This is one of the most important progression systems in the game.

How It Works
Unlock cards using gems and then spend more gems to upgrade them for stronger effects
Priority Upgrades - Early Game
  • Tip Jar: Increases the chance of receiving tips
  • Remote / Pickaxe: Helps progression in Adventure maps and boosts income
  • TV: Lengthens ad boosts you activate
Important: Vault boosts stack with other income sources and speed up city completions overall. Prioritize upgrading your vault early!

Gear System

Gear increases your total profit %, walk speed, and food making speed. This is crucial for fast city progression.

Equip Smart
Equip the highest rarity items you have (Epic > Rare > Common)
Upgrade Using Duplicates
Upgrade gear using duplicates to boost its multiplier
Focus on Profit % Early
Focus on profit % bonuses early game — this speeds up city clears the most
⚠️ Don't Waste Gear
Don't waste strong gear by donating it unless you have better replacements

Gear Builds

Gear (clothes & accessories) gives key stat bonuses (profit %, speed, effects) when equipped. Gear is obtained mostly from boxes, events, club rewards, and adventures.

Meta Build Priorities
  • 1st Priority: Profit % items — boosts overall city coin gains
  • 2nd Priority: Speed & Walk Speed — improves completion speed
  • 3rd Priority: Situational bonuses (like food value boosts)
Pro Tip: CHECK THE SPREADSHEET AT THE END OF THE GUIDE TO SEE THE BEST BUILDS FOR YOUR CITY. IT WILL HELP YOU A LOT IN YOUR PROGRESSION!

Pets

What Pets Do
Pets give extra All Profit % special bonuses and small mechanics (like auto tip collection)
How to Get Pets
Open Pet Eggs (from Pet Boxes or merged pets)
Pet Rarities
Common → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Ultimate
Pet Strategy
Stack the pet food for bigger eggs like purple and legendary eggs and to level up your big pets. You can also merge pets to increase their rarity and use them as merge pets to upgrade stronger pets like the panda

Clubs

Clubs unlock at City 7 and provide significant progression bonuses.

Why Join a Club
Join an active club to earn seasonal rewards like gems and boxes
How to Progress
Donate duplicate or low-tier gear to gain Club XP
Club Benefits
Higher club level = better milestone rewards for everyone
⚠️ Avoid Inactive Clubs
Progress will be much slower in inactive clubs
Important: Join the unofficial Eatventure Discord server to find a good active club: https://discord.gg/eatventure

Gems Strategy

Save Early Game
Try saving them up early in the game as you will need them to upgrade your vault and buy the event boxes
Avoid Shop Boosts
Do not use gems to buy boost in the shop (the 2x and 5x ones)
Smart Boost Strategy
If you do buy the boosts, try watching an ad before and then buy them both at the same time to boost your total profit multiplier by a lot

Events

Events aren't much important for us — only good to spend your gems on the special boxes in the shop when they are active.

Unique Items
Each event has their own unique blueprint items — check the spreadsheet at the end of the guide for more info

Fast Completion Tips

🚀 Permanent 2x Boost
I recommend buying the permanent 2x boost — you will see it will give you a huge boost and faster completion
🎯 Reach City 7 Fast
Try reaching the level City 7 which will unlock the clubs and will boost your city completions by a lot
💎 Starter Pack (Optional)
There is a starter pack which you unlock early in the game, costing around $15. Buying it will speed your progression a lot but will become almost useless at city 60-70

Crediting Time

Credits usually happen instantly

Can take a few hours sometimes depending on the steps
