Why You Should Do This Offer

This is one of the easiest finance offers with extremely fast returns:

  • Very quick signup process

Beginner Friendly: This offer requires no crypto experience and is perfect for anyone looking for quick, guaranteed profits.

How the Offer Works

The steps are extremely simple:

  1. Sign up on Uphold with a valid email address and phone number
  2. Complete KYC verification
    • Upload a government-issued ID
    • Take a selfie or short video verification
  3. Deposit approximately $15
  4. Buy or trade $15 worth of any cryptocurrency
  5. Wait for the offer to credit

Important Notes

Critical: KYC is mandatory to withdraw funds and to receive your offerwall reward.
  • Always deposit around $15 or slightly more to cover trading and withdrawal fees
  • Depositing the full $15 is strongly recommended because withdrawal fees apply and anything lower may prevent you from cashing out your remaining balance
  • Keep screenshots of signup, verification, deposit, and trade confirmation in case support is required
  • Use real personal information to avoid verification issues

Payment and Purchase Details

You may buy any cryptocurrency supported by Uphold.

Recommended Options

  • USDT or USDC (stablecoins)

  • ETH
Pro Tip: Stablecoins are ideal due to minimal price fluctuations.

Crediting Times

The offer is known for very fast tracking:

If not credited after 24 hours, contact support with your proof screenshots.

Withdrawal Information
