Purchase System

Important Platform Difference:

This game features repeatable purchase packs on the $4.99 and $9.99 purchases (iOS only). Completing each of the purchases for the whole month on iOS will profit you $120 in additional cashback and get you a lot of progress in resources and speedups.

$4.99 Purchases - Priority Order

Jade Blessing (First Mandatory Purchase)
This is your first and most important $4.99 purchase.

Jade Blessing is the single most important purchase in the game. It provides a 5% boost to construction and activates auto combat, which are both crucial for progression.

  • Activates auto combat
  • Reduces construction time by 20 minutes
  • Provides an extra drop while slaying monsters
  • Auto combat ensures you don't have to play the game 100% of the time
  • Makes it a more casual game with less grind

$4.99 Purchase Priority (iOS)

  • Monthly Purchases - Start with these after Jade Blessing (highest value, repeatable on iOS)
  • Weekly Purchases - Slightly weaker but still offer good resources or gold (repeatable on iOS)
  • Daily Purchases - Usually least cost-effective but still profitable (repeatable on iOS)

Gold Blessings subscription is the single most important purchase in the game. It provides a 5% boost to construction and unlocks offline auto trades, which are crucial for progression.

$50 Purchase - Gemstones

Gemstones - Flexible In-Game Currency
Buy Gemstones to have more flexibility in your purchases

Gemstones are a flexible in-game currency that can be used to purchase various resources and speedups, providing you with more control over your progression.

  • Gemstones replace direct card payments in the shop
  • Can be used to purchase Gold when you run low
  • Can be used to buy Accelerations (speedups) for faster progression
  • Provides flexibility to spend on what you need most at any given moment
  • Useful for adjusting your strategy based on current game requirements

Recommended Use: Save Gemstones for critical moments when you need a specific resource urgently, especially gold or construction speedups during the final levels.

Level Guide

Levels 1-10 (1-2 days)

Levels 1-10 are simple and will take you a day and a half to complete.

Strategy:

  1. Simply build all the buildings you need
  2. Upgrade your flagship to increase the amount of buildings you can have
  3. After purchasing all initial packs, you should have enough speedups and resources to reach level 10 without spending gems
IMPORTANT: Make sure to collect quest rewards for building buildings so you can get some of the resources back!

Priority Buildings:

  • Storage Buildings - Make as many as possible
    • Increase supplies capacity
    • Offline Trading generates more gold with more storage
    • Lowers the amount of time you need to play in the long run
  • Oarmaster - Good to have multiple
    • Allows your ship to move faster
    • Makes trading routes faster

Join a Powerful Guild:

  • Guilds (called "gangs") can attack and capture ports
  • Captured ports give everyone bonuses
  • Eventually the map will be owned by ~5 top guilds
  • Best to be in one of those from the start

Levels 11-20 (Notable Slowdown)

This is where the game can slow down A LOT because you start to need A LOT of gold to complete flagship level ups.

Gold Strategy - Black Market Routes:

You can earn 5-10 million a day completing black market routes:

  1. Go to a black market port (preferably highest level 3+)
  2. Purchase all the black market supplies
  3. Use a teleport item to instantly appear next to an ally halfway across the map
  4. Sell the supplies instantly for 150k profit
  5. Do this a few times a day and you'll be rich
Guild Coordination: If you can coordinate with a Guild mate, it's best for teleporting and trading routes.

Power Development:

  • Focus all Ship Traits and Astronomy research on Ship Power
  • Makes auto combats easier
  • You can hit higher level targets

Exploration:

  • Explore as much of the map as possible
  • Unlock as many ports as you can
  • Increases range of trade routes = more money

Daily Energy Usage:

  • Use all energy every day with auto combats from Jade Blessings
  • Gives adventuring XP and gold

Resource Management:

  • Game will slow down heavily at this point
  • Offline trading should sustain your gold usage
  • Use daily $4.99 purchases on Gems (iOS only for daily repetition)
  • Turn gems into GOLD if needed

Levels 21-30 (Maximum Slowdown - ~10 days)

The slow down becomes real. Every upgrade requires 10-30 million gold.

Why It Won't Be Difficult For You:

As long as you do everything from previous levels:

  • You'll have offline trading active 24/7
  • You'll have accumulated gold from black market
  • You'll have resources from all purchase packs

Daily Routine:

  1. Log in daily
  2. Complete your daily missions
  3. Purchase your $4.99 and $9.99 packs (Only iOS)
  4. Upgrade all the buildings you need
  5. Manage your trading and routes

Estimated Time:

  • Approximately 10 days of casual daily play
  • After a couple weeks you'll have an easy $500 profit!

Special Events and Bonuses:

  • Keep an eye out for special events with additional rewards
  • Some events can significantly accelerate progress
  • Take advantage of temporary bonuses when available

Final Tips and Tricks

Maximize Your Profit:

  1. Don't skip any daily purchases if you want to maximize cashback (iOS only for daily repetition)
  2. Keep offline trading active at all times
  3. Join an active guild from the beginning
  4. Constantly explore to unlock new trade routes

Time Management:

  • With Jade Blessing and offline trading, this game becomes very casual
  • You don't need to be constantly connected
  • 15-30 minute daily sessions are enough after initial levels

Gem Spending Priorities:

  1. Gold (if you run out for upgrades)
  2. Construction speedups (if you have free queues)
  3. Resources (only if absolutely necessary)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I complete this without purchases?

Not recommended. Offline trading and speedups are essential to complete in a reasonable time.

How long should I play each day?

After the initial levels, 15-30 minutes daily are enough thanks to auto combat and offline trading.

What if I run out of gold?

Use the gems from daily packs to buy gold. Offline trading will also provide you with gold constantly.

Is it necessary to be in a top guild?

Not mandatory, but it helps significantly with port bonuses and support for black market routes.
